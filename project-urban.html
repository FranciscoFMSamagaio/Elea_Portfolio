Project

City Atrium

An urban intervention that brings a calm, sculptural atrium into an energetic civic district.

City Atrium acts as an anchor for public life, combining transparent gathering spaces with offices above. The scheme emphasizes continuity between street, interior and roof terraces.

  • Location: Porto, Portugal
  • Year: 2024
  • Type: Urban Renewal
