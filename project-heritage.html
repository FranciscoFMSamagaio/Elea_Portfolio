Heritage Pavilion

A sensitive cultural pavilion woven into an existing historic setting.

The Heritage Pavilion is a subtle addition to an existing landscape, respecting context while offering an open exhibition space and contemplative courtyard. The design balances old and new through material restraint and spatial clarity.

  • Location: Cascais, Portugal
  • Year: 2025
  • Type: Cultural Conservation
