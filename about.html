Elea Gomes

About

Elea Gomes is an architect who values calm forms and compelling light.

Rooted in material dialogue, practice explores the quiet beauty of everyday buildings and urban spaces.

Portrait by Elea Gomes

Design philosophy

Architecture is a framework for daily life, defined by atmosphere, proportion, and sensory detail. Projects are conceived through careful spatial sequences, natural material palettes, and an emphasis on sunlight.

Process

Sketching, physical modeling and digital studies are combined to test scale, texture and structure. Each proposal is refined through iterative diagrams, tactile models, and collaborative review.

Intent

To make architecture that feels composed yet alive, where every room, façade, and passage speaks with quiet clarity.
