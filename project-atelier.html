Project

Studio House

A compact atelier-residence designed around a courtyard, generous north light, and material intimacy.

The Studio House is conceived as a calm urban retreat with an internal garden, expressive timber surfaces, and moments of layered daylight. The plan arranges living, working and sleeping around a central courtyard to create varied visual connections.

  • Location: Lisbon, Portugal
  • Year: 2025
  • Type: Residential Atelier
